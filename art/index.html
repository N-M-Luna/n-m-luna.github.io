I'm Luna,

dipper of the watercolor brush and wielder of the ink nib.

Science fiction, math, tattoos, and dancing are other interests which find their way to my art.
Currently based in Northern California, but originally from Puerto Rico.

My favorite mediums to combine are watercolors and ink outlines.
