I'm Luna,

dipper of the watercolor brush and wielder of the ink nib.

Science fiction, math, tattoos, and dancing are other interests which find their way to my art.
Currently based in Northern California, but originally from Puerto Rico.

My favorite mediums to combine are watercolors and ink outlines.

Here is a little bit about me.

I was born and raised in Puerto Rico. I moved to Texas to get my master's in math. Now I'm living in Sacramento, CA. My favorite media is watercolors. I find them extremely challenging but equally satisfying.

Sprouting Boot © 2023 is licensed under CC-BY-NC-SA 4.0.

More often than not, I add some ink outlines to my watercolors. Some strong influences are Traditional American Tattoos and punk and rock music. Fun fact: A teenage and impressionable Luna poured over the album jacket for Americana by The Offspring.

Ruin © 2024 is licensed under CC-BY-NC-SA 4.0.

Science fiction in any form is an instant favorite of mine. Some of my favorite writers Orson Scott Card, H. G. Wells, Raybradbury, and anyone writing about Star Trek. I love in particular the mid-century science fiction where space exploration was in vogue and vaguely humanoid household bots did our chores.

Inkbot © 2023 is licensed under CC-BY-NC-SA 4.0.

I started ballet young and was able to enjoy it for ~10 years. I've also danced jazz, salsa, belly dance, modern/contemporary, spanish folk, hip-hop, and pole dance. It's always a pleasure to sketch, draw and paint the shapes that dancers do with their bodies, costumes, and props.

The Roaring Dancer © 2023 is licensed under CC-BY-NC-SA 4.0.

Just being able to have the support to create new interesting illustrations and personal paintings is enough for me. But I would be thrilled to be given the chance to illustrate a book. =)
